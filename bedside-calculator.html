How to use

Enter values for any subset of inputs. Results update live. Each card shows three things:

  • The result with its unit (large, bold).
  • The baseline formula in symbolic form (italic) — always visible so you see what the equation looks like even before filling anything in.
  • The worked substitution (monospace) — the formula with your literal values plugged in, plus the answer. Hidden until inputs are populated.

Ranges and defaults are conservative; placeholder text shows a typical adult value. PBW switches between the men/women formulas when you change the Sex dropdown.
